Yogurt Pancakes

These might just be the best, fluffiest pancakes you could ever make. No meringue required.

Ingredients:

  • 125g All-purpose Flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/4 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 1 tbsp, 3 1/2 tsps Equal Sweetener
  • 3 Large Eggs
  • 125g Yogurt
  • 28g Melter Butter
  • 1/2 tsp Vanilla Extract

Cooking Directions:

  1. Whisk sifted flour and other dry ingredients in a bowl
  2. Beat two the eggs mixed in with vanilla extract, and then mix again with yogurt
  3. Make a well in the dry ingredients bowl, then pour melted butter in
  4. Pour in egg and yogurt mix into the bowl and fold and mix gently, making sure not to overmix
  5. Pre-heat stove to medium-high and pour in three or more tbsps of batter at a time
  6. Flip when edges have set and dried and bubbles have formed on top
  7. Check other side and cook 'til desired doneness
  8. Repeat until batter is out
